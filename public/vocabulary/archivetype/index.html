The physical medium in which the response of a proxy sensor to environmental forcing is recorded and upon which the proxy measurements were made.

Definition

source: PaST

Peat

Definition

a bulk biological material that consists of an unconsolidated combustible deposit of partially carbonized plant remains, common in bogs, fens, and lakes in tundra or periglacial regions (Credit: Smol et al. 2001 Tracking Environmental Change Using Lake Sediments).

source: PaST

Known synonyms
